How to Play

  • 👈👉Move with the arrow keys or by dragging
  • 🍓Catch fruit & veg for points — rarer ones score more
  • 🔥Catch them in a row to build your multiplier (up to ×10)
  • 🍔Catching junk food costs a life
  • ⬇️Letting good food hit the floor costs a life
  • 💗Grab a rare heart for an extra life (up to 5)
  • 🌶️Grab a rare chilli for a 2× speed boost
  • ❤️You start with 3 lives — how long can you last?
